This application is Treasure Map, a way for plotting free stuff posted on craigslist onto a map. Zero to no cost!

The Treasure Map requires the precise name of the stuff's city, without spaces, in order for the url to work. New York is then newyork.
Currently only five cities are supported: Montreal, Toronto, Vancouver, Washington D.C., and New York.
Try: D.C. or New York.
