About

With the treasure map, compare craigslist free stuff postings by location. The distance of a free thing is directly related to its value. There are many treasures to be found, using this application, hopefully, you will find something near you worth giving a new home.

The Treasure Map requires the precise name of the stuff's city, without spaces, in order for the url to work -- it must conform to Craigslist's URL. New York is then newyork. Many cities should work 'out of the box' (likely those with one word names). Currently a handful of cities have been tested, like Montreal, Toronto, Philly and New York. Try: D.C. or somewhere random.
To try a city not listed on the navigation menu, simply enter the city name after the url (treasure.plyp.org). Visit the cities page for a complete list of Craiglist valid city names.


Application Interface

Type your location into the url:

treasure.plyp.org/location
treasure.plyp.org/location/map
treasure.plyp.org/location/map/x
The default map loads 10 items.

These stuffs are marked by circles. The newer the posting, the bigger the circle.

The darker the border, the greater the overlap.

Plotting a BUNCH of stuff (free stuffs) is doable, but the map will take many seconds to load. Above, 65 items loaded in 25 seconds.


The program which calculates the coordinates for stuff-locations will sometimes crash. If you receive an error (either Internal Error 500, or a traceback error "Geocoder Service Timeout"), try refreshing the page.
