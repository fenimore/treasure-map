About

With the treasure map, compare craigslist free stuff postings by location. The distance of a free thing is inversely related to its value. There are many treasures to be found, using this application, hopefully, you will find something near you worth giving a new home.

Application Interface

Append your location into the url:

/<location>
/<location>/map
/<location>/<limit>

The default map loads 25 items.

These stuffs are marked by circles. The newer the posting, the bigger the circle (and the darker the border, the greater the overlap).
